About Me

I recently graduated from Texas Tech University in 2022 with a Bachelor of Science in Mathematics and a minor in Physics. I was born in Plainview, Texas (A small town between Amarillo and Lubbock). I later moved to Lubbock, Texas, to attend Lubbock Cooper High School, where I graduated with honors. My interests widely vary depending on the time of day, including but not limited to data science, applications to machine learning, finance, and solving ordinary and partial differential equations. My website will eventually reflect this as I slowly add completed projects. My work experience primarily revolves around food service, with my current job being a Cook at H-E-B. This experience has honed my ability to work with others as a team to achieve goals within a tight deadline and to work in fast-paced, high-stress environments. I also have extensive experience providing excellent customer service. At the bottom are links to my Github, where I post completed projects and my LinkedIn page.
